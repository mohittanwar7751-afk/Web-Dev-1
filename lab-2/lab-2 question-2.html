COLD DRINKS BRANDS

Mountain Dew is a popular citrus-flavored soft drink known for its bold, tangy, and refreshing taste. Originally created in the 1940s, it has become a favorite among young people for its energetic image and adventurous branding. The drink is often associated with thrill, excitement, and extreme sports, thanks to its marketing campaigns that emphasize daring lifestyles. With its bright green color and high caffeine content, Mountain Dew is not just a beverage but also a symbol of energy and enthusiasm for those who seek a bold refreshment.


Pepsi is one of the world's most popular carbonated soft drinks, known for its sweet, refreshing cola flavor. Created in the late 19th century, it has grown into a global brand that represents youthfulness, energy, and fun. Over the years, Pepsi has introduced many variations, from diet and zero-sugar options to fruity blends, appealing to diverse tastes.

Frooti is one of India's most loved mango-flavored drinks, enjoyed for its sweet and refreshing taste. Launched in 1985, it quickly became a favorite, especially among children and young people, for its convenient packaging and rich fruity flavor. Made with real mango pulp, Frooti is often associated with fun, freshness, and a burst of tropical energy.
